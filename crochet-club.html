Crochet Club

Crochet & Coffee's Weekly Club


To celebrate our one year anniversary in July 2022, we set up our walking club. Every Monday, Wednesday and Saturday we all gather at the café, grab a brew to take with us and take our dogs for a walk to the nearby park. The walk is about 3km total and we have great fun doing them.


Monday and Wednesday walks start at 6:30pm and Saturday walks start at 1pm. Either Christine or Tom will join the group while the other stays in the café and the walks go ahead regardless of the weather. It's free to join unless you fancy taking a brew with you in which case walk club members get 50% off their drink on days that they walk with us. Fill out the form below and get involved!
